About Us

Welcome to Hungry Man, where the aroma of sizzling burgers and the comforting scent of homemade pies beckon you in. Nestled in the heart of Nashville, Tennessee, this beloved eatery is the brainchild of Dave, a seasoned restaurateur with a passion for classic American cuisine. At 73 years young, Dave's culinary journey began decades ago, fueled by a love for hearty, soul-satisfying dishes. Hungry Man prides itself on serving up generous portions of nostalgia, from juicy burgers dripping with cheese to crispy fried chicken and fluffy biscuits. With its cozy diner ambiance and friendly staff, Hungry Man promises not just a meal, but an experience reminiscent of simpler times.
